What I do

Most clients hire me for a mix of these. Tap any to see how I work it.

I plan, run, and optimize paid programs across LinkedIn, Google, and Meta. Account structure, audience strategy, creative testing, and turning spend into qualified pipeline instead of just clicks.

I audit where a brand can become more visible, in traditional search and inside AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity, then build the keyword and content plan to get there.

For new products, segments, or markets. I help founders figure out who they are really for, what message lands, which channels are worth showing up in, and what the first 90 days should look like.

I build the reporting that tells you what is working. Unified scorecards that pull conversion, cost per lead, and channel performance into one place, often automated so it runs without the manual spreadsheet work.

For teams between hires, or founders who want a senior marketer in the seat before committing full-time. I run the function, manage vendors and agencies, and report into leadership.
