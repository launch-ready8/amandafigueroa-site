The challenge

Cost per form fill above $1,500.

The team had paid search infrastructure in place: campaigns were live, segmented by intent, and producing inbound leads. The bottleneck was cost. In the month before we started working together, the average cost per conversion across search ads sat above $1,500 per form fill. For the program to scale, that number had to come down.

The approach

Where the levers were.

The campaign foundation was solid, so the work was to find where small adjustments would compound.

We restructured campaigns around buyer intent tiers: high-intent terms got their own budget, broader research-phase terms got their own creative. Then we rewrote the landing pages so they spoke directly to the person typing the query, in their language, with one obvious next step.

From there, we cut the bottom 30% of keywords by spend, tightened audience signals, and built a rhythm of three new ad concepts per week. Every Friday, a one-page report went out: what we shipped, what worked, what was getting cut the following week.

The results

A 70% drop in CPA on the same budget.
