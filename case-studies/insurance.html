Case Study · Insurance

Cutting cost-per-lead 49% with a referral program.

An NJ insurance agency built an inbound channel that didn't have to compete on price.

The challenge

Their best customers weren't shopping on price.

The agency had been buying leads from aggregator vendors, each one resold to 10+ other agencies all racing to call first and undercut on price. They wanted exclusive leads: people actively shopping, but not already on every competitor's call list.

Their best-fit customers (the ones who stayed, paid premium, referred their friends) rarely came from aggregator lists. Those clients came in through life events, and the first people they called were loan officers and real estate agents.

The approach

Formalize the channel that already existed.

We designed and launched a structured referral program for loan officers across New Jersey: enrollment workflow, partner-facing materials, a tracking system that integrated with the agency's existing CRM, and a monthly cadence to keep partners warm. The program gave loan officers a clear, low-friction way to send their clients to the agency.

Built and launched in under 90 days. No new ad spend.

The results

Inbound that didn't depend on ad spend.
